# rosary-ios

**Pray the Rosary on your iPhone. Free. No account. No ads.**

`3 Mystery Sets · Latin & English · 7 Novenas · Bead-by-Bead · Offline`

A native iOS app for praying the Holy Rosary — step by step through every bead, with full prayer texts in English and Latin. A free, open-source alternative to Hallow, built for traditional Catholics.

## Features

- **All three mystery sets** — Joyful, Sorrowful, and Glorious, with daily suggestions based on traditional day assignments
- **Bead-by-bead prayer** — swipe forward to advance, swipe back to go back; haptic feedback on every bead, stronger at decade transitions
- **Latin toggle** — switch between English and Latin prayer texts at any point (EN/LA button in toolbar)
- **Full prayer texts** — Sign of the Cross, Apostles' Creed, Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, Fatima Prayer, Hail Holy Queen, and closing prayers
- **Visual bead progress** — animated indicator showing position within each decade
- **Mystery meditations** — each decade displays the mystery name and Scripture reference (Douay-Rheims)
- **Completion tracking** — daily and all-time Rosary counts
- **Seven novenas** — Sacred Heart, Miraculous Medal, St. Joseph, St. Jude, St. Thérèse, Our Lady of Perpetual Help, and the 54-Day Rosary Novena with phase and progress tracking
- **Novena intentions** — record your intention when starting a novena; daily prayer text guides each day
- **Dark liturgical theme** — deep purple/black with gold accents
- **No network required** — fully offline; no account, no tracking

## Mystery Sets

| Set | Days | Mysteries |
|-----|------|-----------|
| Joyful | Monday & Thursday | Annunciation, Visitation, Nativity, Presentation, Finding in Temple |
| Sorrowful | Tuesday & Friday | Agony in Garden, Scourging, Crowning with Thorns, Carrying the Cross, Crucifixion |
| Glorious | Wednesday, Saturday & Sunday | Resurrection, Ascension, Descent of Holy Ghost, Assumption, Coronation of Mary |

## Novenas

| Novena | Days | Patron |
|--------|------|--------|
| 54-Day Rosary Novena | 54 | Blessed Virgin Mary |
| Sacred Heart | 9 | Sacred Heart of Jesus |
| Miraculous Medal | 9 | Blessed Virgin Mary |
| St. Joseph | 9 | St. Joseph |
| St. Jude | 9 | St. Jude Thaddeus |
| St. Thérèse | 9 | St. Thérèse of Lisieux |
| Our Lady of Perpetual Help | 9 | Blessed Virgin Mary |

The 54-Day Rosary Novena rotates through Joyful, Sorrowful, and Glorious mysteries across 27 days of petition followed by 27 days of thanksgiving.

## Requirements

- iOS 17.0+
- Xcode 15+
- Swift 5.9+

## Build

```sh
git clone https://github.com/ecclesia-dev/rosary-ios.git
cd rosary-ios
open HolyRosary.xcodeproj
```

Build and run on simulator or device. No external dependencies, no package manager required.
