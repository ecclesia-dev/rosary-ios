## 3. Description (4000 char max)

```
Pray the Rosary bead by bead. All fifteen traditional mysteries — Joyful, Sorrowful, and Glorious — with full prayer texts in English and Latin. Free. No ads, no accounts, no tracking.

Swipe forward to advance through each bead. Every prayer is written out in full — Apostles' Creed, Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, and the Fatima Prayer. You never need to pray from memory unless you want to.

A simple, traditional Rosary app. Nothing between you and the prayer.

MYSTERIES

• Joyful Mysteries — Monday & Thursday
• Sorrowful Mysteries — Tuesday & Friday
• Glorious Mysteries — Wednesday, Saturday & Sunday

Each decade displays the mystery name and a Scripture reference from the Douay-Rheims Bible. The app suggests the correct mystery set for today based on traditional day assignments.

PRAYER EXPERIENCE

• Bead-by-bead navigation — swipe to advance, swipe back to return
• Haptic feedback on every bead, stronger at decade transitions
• Visual bead progress indicator within each decade
• Full prayer texts — Sign of the Cross, Apostles' Creed, Our Father, Hail Mary, Glory Be, Fatima Prayer, Hail Holy Queen, and closing prayers
• Latin toggle — switch between English and Latin at any point

NOVENAS

Seven novenas with daily prayer texts and progress tracking:

• 54-Day Rosary Novena (27 days petition + 27 days thanksgiving)
• Sacred Heart of Jesus
• Miraculous Medal
• St. Joseph
• St. Jude
• St. Thérèse of Lisieux
• Our Lady of Perpetual Help

Record your prayer intention when starting a novena. Your intention is stored only on your device — never transmitted or shared.

COMPLETION TRACKING

• Daily and all-time Rosary completion counts
• Novena day tracking with phase indicators
• No streaks, no badges, no gamification — just a simple record of your prayer

PRIVACY

This app collects no data. It makes no network connections. No analytics, no advertising, no third-party code. Your prayer history and intentions stay on your device and nowhere else.

WHAT THIS APP IS

A traditional Catholic Rosary app built for prayer, not engagement metrics. The fifteen mysteries. The prayers of the Church. Nothing added, nothing removed.

WHAT THIS APP IS NOT

Not a meditation app with ambient sounds. No Luminous Mysteries — this follows the traditional form. No social features, no subscription, no upsells. Just the Rosary.
```
