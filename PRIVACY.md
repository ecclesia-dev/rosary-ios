# Privacy Policy — Holy Rosary

**Effective Date:** 2026-02-27
**App Name:** Holy Rosary
**Developer:** Ecclesia Dev
**Contact:** privacy@ecclesiadev.com

---

## Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal data. No information is gathered, transmitted, or shared with anyone.

## On-Device Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

- **Rosary history** — The date and mystery set each time you complete a Rosary.
- **Novena progress** — Which novena you are praying, the start date, completed days, and your prayer intention.

This data never leaves your device. It is not synced to any server, cloud service, or third party.

## Prayer Intentions

If you enter a prayer intention for a novena, it is stored only in your device's local storage. It is never transmitted, read by anyone else, or shared in any way.

## Network Activity

This app makes no network connections. It functions entirely offline. No internet connection is required or used at any time.

## Third-Party Services

This app uses no third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or crash reporting tools.

## Tracking

This app does not track you. There is no analytics, no device fingerprinting, no usage monitoring, and no advertising identifiers.

## Children's Privacy

This app does not collect data from anyone, including children. It is safe for use by all ages.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated, the revised version will be posted here with a new effective date.

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact: privacy@ecclesiadev.com
